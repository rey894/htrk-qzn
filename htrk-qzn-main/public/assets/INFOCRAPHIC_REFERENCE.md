### Economic Powerhouse
- **Sugar:** BUSCO Sugar Milling Company, thousands of jobs
- **Agribusiness:** Del Monte Philippines, DAVCO, Ranchu Montalvan cattle
- **Labor Force:** 73.92% participation; 85.36% in farming/forestry/fishing
- **Mother Tongue (bar chart):** Binukid 68,009 | Hiligaynon 6,498 | Ilocano 1,338 | Maranao 1,060 | Tagalog 471 | Manobo 735

### Demographic Profile
- 85% of population below age 30
- Poverty incidence: 31.96% (2021), down from 50% in 2015

### Tourism & Natural Wonders
- **Kiokong White Rock Wall** – 560 ft rock face, vertical bivouac
- **Blue Water Cave** – Along Pulangi River, distinct water color
- **Overview Nature and Culture Park** – Palacapae, panoramic view

### Historical Milestones
- **1966:** R.A. 4802 – Municipality of Quezon (from Barangay Kiokong)
- **1974:** BUSCO – sugar economy, first banking
- **2008:** Del Monte fresh fruit packing house, Barangay San Jose
