# Site Testing Feedback Form

Use this template when creating a Google Form for collecting feedback on https://quezon.haturiko.net

## Form Title
**Quezon Municipal Website - Site Testing Feedback**

## Form Description
We appreciate your feedback to improve our website. This short form helps us understand your experience.

---

## Recommended Questions

| # | Question | Type | Options / Notes |
|---|----------|------|-----------------|
| 1 | **Device used** | Multiple choice | Desktop, Mobile (phone), Tablet, Other |
| 2 | **Browser** | Short answer or dropdown | Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Edge, Other |
| 3 | **Which page(s) had issues?** | Checkbox or Short answer | Home, About, Governance, Services, Investment, Tourism, Contact, Other (specify) |
| 4 | **Describe the issue** | Paragraph | Free text for page-specific problems |
| 5 | **Loading speed** | Linear scale (1–5) | 1 = Very slow, 5 = Very fast |
| 6 | **Accessibility issues** | Paragraph | Any difficulties with navigation, reading, or using the site (e.g., screen readers, font size) |
| 7 | **Content clarity** | Linear scale (1–5) | 1 = Unclear, 5 = Very clear |
| 8 | **General feedback** | Paragraph | Suggestions, compliments, or other comments |

---

## After Creating the Form

1. In Google Forms: **Settings** → **Responses** → Enable "Collect email addresses" (optional).
2. Click **Send** → **Link** to copy the shareable link.
3. For embedding: **Send** → **<>** (embed) to get the iframe code.
